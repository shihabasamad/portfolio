Irish Rail Passenger Inquiry Reporting

Excel Reporting 100+ inquiries

Conducted an on-ground analysis of 100+ passenger inquiries to identify high-frequency service issues and propose targeted improvements to reduce recurring queries.

Problem

Recurring passenger questions were increasing pressure on frontline staff and slowing service communication during busy periods.

Approach

  • Recorded and categorised inquiries by topic, location, and time patterns.
  • Summarised frequency drivers and mapped root causes (e.g., delay updates, platform confusion).
  • Proposed improvements focused on signage and digital communication tools.

Key Insights

  • Delays and platform confusion were the highest-frequency categories.
  • Communication gaps were strongest for the 20–45 tech-savvy demographic.
  • Targeted signage and digital tools could reduce repeat inquiries.

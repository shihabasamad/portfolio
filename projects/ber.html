Building Energy Rating (BER) Performance Analysis

Power BI SQL Excel 1M+ dwellings 200+ features

Performed large-scale analysis of BER performance to identify key BER and CO2 drivers and develop region-specific retrofit recommendations for worst-performing dwellings.

Problem

Energy performance varied widely across dwellings; stakeholders needed clarity on the main drivers and where retrofits would have the highest impact.

Approach

  • Cleaned and validated BER dataset fields (area, build era, dwelling type, insulation metrics).
  • Segmented performance and flagged worst-performing cohorts for targeted action.
  • Built KPI views linking build era, size, and insulation factors to BER and CO2 outcomes.

Key Insights

  • Insulation and build era were major drivers of BER and CO2 performance.
  • Worst-performing 20% formed clear target cohorts for retrofit prioritisation.
  • Recommendations varied by dwelling type and region, supporting more efficient planning.
